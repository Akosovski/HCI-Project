Operators

Team Rainbow provides the best individual who are able
to commit dangerous missions and situations.
Equipped with high tech equipment and weapons,
also able to conduct specialized combat tactics
in many environment.

General Purpose Units

Standard Operators apply standard rules of engagement.
For each phase of the match, choose one of the operators, each with their own specific
gadget and playstyle. Build the best team possible out of operators available
by selecting operators that complete each other.

Special Purpose Units

Special Operators often referred as dedicated individuals that
performs combat tactics differently from standard units with their own unique skills, gadgets and equipments.
There are three combat operators included with the newly launched Events that follows. Each of them represents
the variety of gameplay available in Rainbow Seven Siege. Use their specialized equipments to
bring the edge of your team in the field.
